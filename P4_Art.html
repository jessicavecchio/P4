Why my art is important to me...


Ever since I was young, I loved art. Some of those included sculpting and painting but photography was something I was really drawn to. I collected photographs and took multiple photography classes. During high school, I even wanted to become a photographer. I loved the ability to be able to take an image of something happening in real time and save it forever. Sometimes I stumble upon different types of media that I enjoy as well like the others mentioned above, but photography will always have a special place in my heart.
